Roadmap for 2026: Technology to People

Cloud & AI Adoption

Continuing platform investments and migrating agencies to the cloud to unlock innovation and save money.

Cybersecurity Focus

Identifying risks, accelerating remediation, shifting to cloud-centric security, and driving local cyber progress.

Strategic Sourcing

Improving infrastructure and procurement to realize value faster, including the $1B MSI contract recompetes.

Intelligent Apps

Focusing on web-native solutions, shared AI agents, and expanding the enterprise App Store to avoid re-work.

Data Over Applications

Prioritizing the data needed to produce mission outcomes, viewing applications simply as a means to the data.

Fiscal Discipline & Talent

Holding costs stable, replacing legacy tech, and building up top IT talent within VITA and statewide.
